+===========+===============+===============+============+===============+============+==============+========================+=================+
|    UVC    |       5.0v    |  USB 1.1/2.0  |  UVC 1.5   |High/Full-Speed|    MJPEG   |   1280*720   |         1024           |  不支持DRI字段  |
+-----------+---------------+---------------+------------+---------------+------------+--------------+------------------------+-----------------+
|    UAC    |       5.0v    |  USB 1.1/2.0  |  UAC 1.0   |High/Full-Speed|     ALL    |      N       |         1024           |                 |
